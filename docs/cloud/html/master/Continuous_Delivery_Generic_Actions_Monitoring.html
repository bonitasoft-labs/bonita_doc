Monitoring a job

Last job result information

The result of the last job is displayed in next to the job:

  • : Job is in progress
  • : Last job was successful
  • : Last job was unsuccessful

Example

Console Output

You can check the different actions and logs done in a job throught the "console output". This output can be view during or after the job execution.

  1. Open the corresponding job by clicking on its title:
  1. Open the corresponding job instance by click on its ID in the main list or side list
  1. On side side panel, click on the "Console Output" entry:
  1. You will see all the different actions and logs done during the job:
